Negative Effects

Having a messy living area increases the chance of sickness through bateria, allergies, rotten food, or even insects or rodents

While not cleaning may seem like a time-saver, it actually adds to more time wasted through looking for things, lack of concentration, and when you eventually have to clean up

A dirty room leads to an increase in stress and frustration, hurting your mental health

Poor concentration is another negative effect, as being in an untidy place causes you to focus on too many things, hurting your effectiveness and success on what you really need to focus on

71% of roommates argue over the correct way and how often to clean, and this causes stress on relationships. It also doesn't look too good on dates

Having a dirty room leads to poor eating choices, since everything used in a good meal is dirty. This also plays into a lower overall health

What can you do?

There are many different ways how we can clean and stay tidy, without it being too much of a hassle. Here are some ideas!

Clean as you cook! After each meal, make sure the counters are washed off, equipment is put away, and the dishes are washed

Each morning after getting out of bed, make it! There is not a better feeling after getting home from a long day and having a neat bed waiting

Create an organization system that works for you, with labels if needed. Have a place for shoes, clothes, etc. Use the drawers that you have!

Have 1 day a week (or more often if needed), to go through the apartment to clean anything big, such as the bathroom. You can also use this day for laundry

Don't allow any exceptions! If you allow yourself to leave one pair of shoes in the living room, soon all your shoes will be there. Be diligent about staying clean!
